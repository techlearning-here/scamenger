# Scamenger — Web Traffic & Awareness Plan

**Goal:** Build sustained traffic to scamenger.com through social media, community content, and partnerships.  
**Intent:** Awareness + attribution (UTM) so we can measure what drives sign-ups, report submissions, and return visits.

**Principles:**
- **No paid marketing.** All reach is organic (owned channels, community, SEO).
- **Own accounts only.** All content is published on Scamenger’s own social and YouTube accounts — no paid placements or influencer buys.
- **Channels as revenue.** Our owned channels (e.g. YouTube Partner Program, in-stream ads, platform monetization where eligible) are an additional source of income alongside the website.

---

## 1. Strategy Overview

| Pillar | Activities | Primary channels |
|--------|------------|------------------|
| **Short-form video** | Awareness clips (15–60 sec) | Facebook, Instagram, Threads, Twitter/X, TikTok |
| **Community & stories** | Incident stories, news refs, Q&A | Reddit, Quora (with links to scamenger.com) |
| **Long-form & authority** | Body cam–based stories, victim interviews, explainers | YouTube, blog, newsletter |
| **SEO & discoverability** | Location pages, answer-style content, refresh | Site + Google/Bing |
| **Partnerships & PR** | Media, bloggers, community | Links, mentions (institutional partners later when established) |
| **Measurement** | UTM, source tracking, content calendar | Analytics, Search Console |

## 2. Team Responsibilities

Assume a small team; roles can be combined or outsourced.

### 2.1 Content Lead

- **Owns:** Content calendar, story selection, script/outline for videos and long-form.
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Choose weekly themes (e.g. romance scams, tax season, holiday fraud).
  - Approve use of body cam transcripts, victim interviews, and news references (rights, consent, tone).
  - Ensure all content points to scamenger.com with correct UTM and clear CTA (e.g. “Report at scamenger.com”).
- **Deliverables:** Weekly content plan, script/outline for 2–3 short clips + 1 long-form piece (or batch).

### 2.2 Social & Community Manager

- **Owns:** Posting, scheduling, and community replies on social and forums.
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Publish short clips to Facebook, Instagram, Threads, Twitter/X, TikTok (native format per platform).
  - Post stories/news to Reddit and Quora (helpful answers first, link when relevant; follow each platform’s rules).
  - Reply to comments and DMs in brand voice; escalate sensitive or legal questions.
  - Track which posts get reach and clicks (UTM); report weekly.
- **Deliverables:** Daily posts per calendar, weekly summary of top posts and referral traffic.

### 2.3 Video / Production (can be part-time or outsourced)

- **Owns:** Filming, editing, thumbnails, and uploads for short and long-form.
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Turn scripts into 15–60 sec clips for social and 5–15 min YouTube videos.
  - Use approved body cam transcripts and victim stories (with consent) to build “complete stories” (e.g. with AI-assisted scripting; human review required).
  - Optimize for platform (captions, aspect ratio, hook in first 3 sec).
- **Deliverables:** 2–4 short clips per week, 1–2 YouTube videos per month (or as capacity allows).

### 2.4 Partnerships & Outreach

- **Owns:** Relationships with media, bloggers, and community (e.g. consumer or victim-support communities). *Institutional partners (banks, police, regulators, schools) can be pursued later once the site has more traction and credibility.*
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Pitch data/angles to press and bloggers (“trending scam types”, “how to report in [country]”, victim-story roundups).
  - Reach out to relevant communities or creators who cover scams/fraud for link swaps or mentions.
  - Coordinate with Content Lead on messaging and CTAs.
- **Deliverables:** 1–2 new media or community conversations per month; log links and mentions.

### 2.5 Growth / Analytics

- **Owns:** UTM schema, dashboards, and reporting.
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Define and document UTM rules (e.g. `utm_source=reddit`, `utm_medium=post`, `utm_campaign=romance_scam_jan`).
  - Build simple dashboard (GA4 + optional spreadsheet) for traffic by source/campaign.
  - Review Search Console and organic keywords; suggest content topics.
- **Deliverables:** UTM doc, weekly traffic report by source/campaign, monthly SEO/content suggestions.

## 6. UTM Conventions (Example)

- **Source:** `facebook`, `instagram`, `threads`, `twitter`, `tiktok`, `youtube`, `reddit`, `quora`, `newsletter`
- **Medium:** `social`, `post`, `video`, `story`, `email`
- **Campaign:** `awareness_romance_jan`, `report_irs_2025`, `victim_story_1`

**Example link:**  
`https://scamenger.com/report/?utm_source=reddit&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=romance_awareness_jan`

## 7. Legal & Ethical Reminders

- **Body cam / police footage:** Use only from official channels; respect terms of use and copyright; do not edit in a misleading way.
- **Victim interviews:** Use only with clear consent; anonymize unless they agree otherwise; avoid re-traumatizing; frame as awareness, not sensationalism.
- **Reddit/Quora:** No link spam; disclose affiliation where required; follow each community’s self-promotion rules; helpful answer first, link second.
